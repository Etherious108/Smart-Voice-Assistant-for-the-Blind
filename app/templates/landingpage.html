VisionVoice

A Smart Voice Assistant for the Blind

VisionVoice is a transformative tool designed to enhance independence for the visually impaired through advanced AI and Machine Learning. This innovative solution converts visual information into real-time auditory feedback, enabling users to navigate and interact with their surroundings safely and effectively. VisionVoice promotes autonomy, safety, and social inclusion, empowering visually impaired individuals to overcome daily challenges with confidence.
